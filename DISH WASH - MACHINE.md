## Dish Wash - Machine (Commercial Machine)
1. Scraping
   * Remove all solid food waste into a bin
   * Prevents clogging of wash arms
2. Pre-Rinse
   * Use a high-pressure hose to remove grease/sauce
   * Water should be warm but not scalding
3. Loading
   * Place items in the correct rack (pegs for plates, flat for cups)
   * Do not overlap; water must hit all surfaces
4. Temp Check
   * Monitor the "Wash" and "Rinse" gauges
   * High-temp: (80C) for final rinse
   * Low-temp: (50C) with chemical sanitizer
5. Air Dry
   * Remove rack and allow to air dry
   * Never use a cloth/towel to dry
